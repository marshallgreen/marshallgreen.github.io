Perceptual Experience Prototypes

Interactive demos and experimental prototypes exploring motion perception, color perception, visual decision-making, and confidence.

About

I am a cognitive scientist and postdoctoral researcher who designs computer-based visual tasks, perceptual demonstrations, and computational models to understand how people experience visual information.

This portfolio presents selected motion and color perception projects as concept-driven prototypes relevant to experience design, media technology, and perceptual evaluation.
